# Marketing Skills for AI Agents

Source: https://github.com/coreyhaines31/marketingskills

A collection of AI agent skills focused on marketing tasks for SaaS products.

## Available Skills
- **copywriting** - Write/rewrite marketing copy for any page
- **page-cro** - Optimize marketing pages for conversions
- **seo-audit** - Audit and fix SEO issues
- **email-sequence** - Create automated email flows
- **social-content** - Social media content for LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram
- **analytics-tracking** - Set up GA4 and event tracking
- **marketing-ideas** - 139 proven SaaS marketing strategies
- **customer-research** - Conduct and analyze customer research
- **launch-strategy** - Plan product launches
- **pricing-strategy** - Pricing, packaging, monetization

## Triggers
